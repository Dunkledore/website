Wasambo

My home for the next 6 months...

What is the school?

Wasambo Boys High School is a boys boarding school setup by Determined to Develop (D2D). It's funded by school fees and donors of D2D. There is one class of 70 students aged 13-18 and I will be teaching them Maths. The school is still under construction so it's fairly bare at the moment. Hopefully I will see that develop over the next few months.

Initial Impressions

My initial impressions were very overwhelming. I arrived to my house to find an empty concrete room and nothing else. At that point all of the thoughts start coming. Am I making a mistake? Will I manage 6 months? All of these fears were soon overcome though. Getting a bed was easy and once I had unpacked and made things a little more homely, I started to become more comfortable. My host, Ryan, has also been a great help with essentials and lessons.

The Classes

The students are in lessons from 7am until 3pm. They have 45 minutes of maths every morning apart from Monday and Thurday where it is 1.5 hours. To start with we are just doing revision of the subjects they did last term and on thursday we will start Social and Commercial Arithmetic.

The Facilities

The facilities at Wasambo are currently very limited. We have a tap that works half the time so we have to fill up buckets to make sure we don't run out of water. We have an inside toilet that doesn't work so trips to the loo involve going to a hole in the ground outside. We also have a small kitchen which has a sink and a mbawula. A mbawula is a small charcoal stove

Food and Drink

Drinks for me are mostly water and I'm perfectly happy with this. Although the tap doesn't always work, water is always avaliable so I fill up my bottle regularly. I also have these small squash squirting things for the occasional treat but I will be using them sparingly. For food, the 3 other teachers and I came up with a menu and hired a local lady (Nirbanda) to do cooking and cleaning. Sport

The students have extra-curricular activites between 3pm - 5pm everday. This is mostly football. They either train or play matches against local boys or other schools. I will be joining in with shortly. In our house we have a gym which consists or one concrete barbell. It's better than nothing though.
